# **App Name**: Hi/sky Time

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Product Catalog: Showcase watches across various categories (Men, Women, Kids) with detailed product pages including multiple images, pricing, and comprehensive descriptions.
- Intelligent Product Search & Filters: Enable users to efficiently find watches using a keyword search bar and filter options by criteria such as price, type, and style.
- Admin Product Management Dashboard: A secure, role-based admin interface for adding new products, editing existing watch details, uploading and managing product images, assigning categories, and updating stock status.
- Direct Order Call & GPay Options: Integrate prominent 'Call Now' and 'Order via GPay' buttons directly on product pages for streamlined customer order placement according to business preferences.
- AI-powered Watch Recommendations Tool: A generative AI tool to suggest related watches, complementary accessories like straps, and 'customers also viewed' items, thereby enhancing product discovery and engagement for users.
- Responsive User Interface: Ensure the website automatically adapts its layout and elements to provide an optimal browsing and shopping experience on any device, from large desktop screens to smartphones.

## Style Guidelines:

- Brand Primary: Deep Corporate Blue-Gray (#131921), serving as a foundational dark shade for branding elements and potential dark mode backgrounds.
- Secondary UI: Dark Muted Blue-Gray (#232F3E), utilized for distinct sections, headers, or component backgrounds, complementing the primary dark tone.
- Vibrant Accent: Bright Yellow (#FFD814), employed for impactful calls to action, interactive elements, and crucial highlights, providing high contrast.
- Refined Highlight: Muted Gold-Brown (#C9A14A), used for subtle accents or to signify premium elements, adding a sophisticated touch.
- Default Text: Near-Black (#111111), intended primarily for excellent readability on light-themed backgrounds, aligning with the 'Light + Dark hybrid' approach. A contrasting light text color will be applied for elements on dark backgrounds.
- Headline and Body Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly legible appearance, suitable for diverse content across both light and dark UI themes.
- Use minimalist, geometric outline-style icons to maintain a clean and contemporary aesthetic, ensuring consistency with the Amazon-inspired interface for navigation and product actions.
- Implement a flexible, responsive grid-based layout for product listings, featuring a prominent and sticky top navigation bar with an integrated search input, and clear hierarchical content organization to facilitate an intuitive user journey.
- Incorporate subtle and purposeful micro-interactions, such as smooth hover effects for product cards and quick transitions for navigation menus, to enhance user engagement without distraction.
